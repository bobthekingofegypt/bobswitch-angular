RULES

The Ace can be played at any time there is not another trick card in play. Playing an Ace allows you to change the suit to any suit of your choice.
A two of any suit is a "sting" card, it requires the next player in line to either play a two, or pick up two. If the next player plays a two then the total to be picked by the next again player increases by two. This continues until a player cannot play a two.
A four of any suit is a "sting" card, it requires the next player in line to either play a four, or pick up four. If the next player plays a four then the total to be picked by the next again player increases by four. This continues until a player cannot play a four.
After an eight is played the next player either has to play an eight or wait. Waiting causes the player to skip their turn.
A jack causes the order of play to be reversed. In one-on-one games a jack allows the player to play again.
